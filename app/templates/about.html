Thank you for your interest in this project. This project was created for the US Navy's Flight Training Squadron 3 to increase the efficiency of flight schedule distribution. Please feel free to checkout the Github page and provide any bug reports or feature requests either there or directly through the "Report a Bug" link in the navigation bar.

The creator of this project is Capt Richard Johnson of the US Air Force. Capt Johnson holds a Bachelor's Degree in Mathematics from the University of Iowa as well as a Master's of Information and Data Science from the University of California, Berkeley. Additionally, Capt Johnson is a combat pilot with over 1,800 hours in the T-6A/B, T-1A, and C-17A aircraft.
